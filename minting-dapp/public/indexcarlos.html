FAQs

Looking for answers?

Why are devs giving up foundership?

We love tokenomics and economic sustainability, we strongly believe this can be done with decentralization of projects, so we gave away our foundership to build something that lasts.

Genesis CK Holders get to mint in every tier?

Only if they are registered in the corresponding list. If so, they can mint in every tier.

Why mixing CyberKongz and ASCII?

We love CyberKongz and ASCII, it was simple and it fitted our future DAPP tests.

Why Every Mint is 0.1 ETH?

Because this price might be the best to avoid gas fees sales influence and it sparks secondary sales and thus sustainability of the project. Besides, it's nothing for a Genesis Cyberkongz.

Are usage rights included?

Of course.

Are you doing another ASCII Drop?

If people like it, we will do multiple decentralized ASCII Drops, announced on our twitter @AsciiDrops.
